Shenzhen Meile Electronics Co., Ltd.

Shenzhen Meile Electronics Co., Ltd. is a design, development, production, sales of electronic cigarette manufacturing enterprises. Our company is located in the global production base of electronic cigarette -- Shenzhen , since our company (affiliated company) in 2016 entered the electronic cigarette industry, our company has provided safe, healthy and high-quality electronic cigarette products to customers in more than 150 countries or regions around the world.

Shenzhen Meile Electronics Co., Ltd. Has a strong strategic partnership with supply chain partners such as oil manufacturers and oil filling machine manufacturers. From the modulation of oil, to the design, development, manufacture, packaging of electronic cigarrete, as well as providing customers with various types of oil filling machine, we are able to provide customers with all (one-stop) supply chain solutions.
